Infinity Sudoku 9x9

Privacy Policy

Effective date: Nov 27, 2024

Infinity Sudoku 9x9 is designed as a local puzzle app. You can play without creating an account, and we do not use analytics services to track your activity.

Privacy at a glance

No account required

You do not need to register or provide a name, email address, or profile to use the app.

No analytics tracking

We do not use Google Analytics or similar analytics tools in the app or on this support website.

Local app settings

The app may store preferences such as theme, difficulty, score, and feature settings locally on your device.

No personal data sharing

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not sell or share personal information with advertisers.

Full policy

Information we collect

Infinity Sudoku 9x9 does not require personal information. Apple may process App Store purchase or download information under Apple's own terms, but that information is not provided to us as a personal user profile.

Children

The app is suitable for general puzzle play. Parents and guardians should supervise children's online activities and device usage as appropriate.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
