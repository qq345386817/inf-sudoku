How to play "Infinity Sudoku 9x9"

• Choose an empty grid and type in the missing number.

• Tap "Start over" to start a new game.

• Tap Easy, Medium, or Hard to change the level, which correspond to 15, 30, and 45 scores respectively.

• Want a hard copy of the current Sudoku puzzle? Just hit the "🖨️ Print" button and it'll be ready to go!

• When Intelligent Prediction is turned on, there will be fewer alternative answers for each grid and the puzzle will be easier.

• Subscribers get a special perk: double the scores, which become 30, 60, and 90.
